- Info -

I am an assistant professor at the University of Southern California (USC) in Los Angeles. I am broadly interested in dynamical systems, symplectic topology and manifolds. I am generously supported by the NSF Award DMS-2446019 and USI Binational Science Foundation Award 2024157.

I was previously an NSF Postdoctoral Fellow at Princeton-IAS supervised by Helmut Hofer. I received my PhD at UC Berkeley in 2021 supervised by Michael Hutchings .

In Fall 2025, I am teaching Math 535B (Differential Geometry) and coorganizing the USC Topology Seminar.

- Advising -

I am currently open to advising new PhD students. If you are interested, please take a look at this learning guide and email to set up a reading course on one of those topics.

I am also currently open to supervising research for advanced undergraduates interested in geometry. If you are interested, please send an introduction email with a transcript.
