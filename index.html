- Info -

I am an NSF Postdoctoral Fellow jointly at Princeton and IAS. My interests include symplectic topology, dynamical systems, smooth manifolds and symplectic field theory.

I will be a tenure track Assistant Professor at the University of Southern California starting in Fall 2023.

I received my PhD at the University of California, Berkeley in 2021 under the supervision of Michael Hutchings

I was previously awarded the UC President's Postdoctoral Fellowship, the Herb Alexander Prize for outstanding thesis, the NSF Graduate Fellowship, the UC Chancellor's Fellowship, and the Rose Hills Foundation Fellowship.
